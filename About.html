Who are you?

I am a Digital Humanities student at the University of Chicago.


Where have you lived?

I grew up in Shenzhen, China and moved to LA when I was 18. I went to College in Colorado Springs. Waking up to the Pikes Peak was a favorite memory. I had lived in Brazil for 3 months learning Portuguese, and Finland for half a year making stone arts. I spent a year in Monterey studying Translation and Interpretation. Now I travel between Chicago and Nashville.


What do you love?

I love daydreaming, drawing, painting, printmaking, playing the banjo, making other people happy, making stained glass, picking my scattered brain apart, and getting mildly upset at Freudian psychoanalysis.


What are your dreams?

I don't mind being a spy, a chef, or an artist! If not, then I shall remain happy, scattered and useful.
